About Us

Mahmoud’s journey with dogs has started since his childhood, he was considering dogs not only as animals yet he was seeing it as beautiful, loyal, friendly creators, although he has been exposed to a horrible attack from a street dog, yet despite all, this accident has built an incredible courage for him and made him insists more to know how to act with dogs and control their emotions in order to protect their beautiful, peaceful and friendly nature “as he believed always”.


Mahmoud has decided to kick off with his journey by learning more about dogs physiological behavior, so after a short period of working as a private trainer, he got a great opportunity to be trained by “The dog whisperer” - Cesar Millan - wherein he has obtained his magic way of managing not only dog’s behavior yet also human’s canine relationships to both dog owners and dog professionals.


Mahmoud is the only trainer in Egypt to get certified as “Pet Dog Trainer” by the well known trainer over the world “Cesar Millan” and he is keen to replicate same methodology and technic in his academy.


Mahmoud not only got exposed to the best dog trainer in the world yet he was passionate to explore more an more in dogs’ world, so he got trained by Colleen Steckloff and got inspired by her unique techniques in dog rehabilitation and obedience. Mahmoud has continued his mission by establishing his own Academy in 2012 when he enjoyed a great boost in his reputation and became a well-known trainer in Egypt Dog’s community, in Mahmoud Fady Academy he offers everything from puppy training to rehabilitation for severe aggression. Mahmoud also has a great team of experts who are well trained to achieve the academy’s program goals for your dog. Entering the cinema world was a beginning of Mahmoud fame amongst Egyptian celebrities, as Mahmoud has managed the training of many movies’ dogs in the Egyptian cinema, which expanded his network to give trainings to celebrities’ personal dogs. This exposure made Mahmoud a very interesting and important topic to be tackled by the highest reach TV and radio shows, Mahmoud is not only limiting his network to Egypt yet he has also expanded it to train some Hollywood celebrities.
